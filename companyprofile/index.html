Foundit Company Profile

Online job board platform for multi-industry

Founded: 1999 • Location: Hyderabad, India • Status: Acquired

Last updated: November 17, 2024

About the Company

What does Foundit do? Foundit is an online job board platform for multiple industries. It helps users find jobs based on their skills, career goals, job category, location, experience, and salary. Covering sectors like marketing, IT, HR, finance, and legal, it also allows candidates to post resumes and access career tips. Employers can post jobs, and the platform is available on both Google Play and Apple App Store.

Key Metrics

  • Total Funding: $14.9M in 1 round
  • Latest Funding Round: Series A, December 22, 2021 ($14.9M)
  • Post Money Valuation: $88.3M as of August 1, 2022

Funding & Investors

Foundit has raised $14.9M in a single funding round (Series A) and has a post-money valuation of $88.3M. Investors have shown interest due to the platform's strong growth and user base.

Founders & Board of Directors

The company was founded by industry experts who envisioned a platform connecting employers with the right candidates. The board includes leaders with extensive experience in recruitment, technology, and business operations.

Acquisition Details

Foundit was acquired by a leading global organization in 2023 to expand its reach and develop new features for its users.
